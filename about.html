About Simply Revived

Simply Revived began as a small neighbourhood bookstore with the simple aim of rekindling the love of reading and creating a friendly meeting place for the local community.

Our History

Founded in 2015 by two friends who loved books and conversation, Simply Revived began with a small curated selection and a kettle on the stove. Over the years we expanded into a welcoming space with a café corner, local author events, and community initiatives.

Mission

To inspire a love for reading and community by offering a curated selection of books and a cosy space where stories, coffee and connection come together.

Vision

To be the go-to neighbourhood bookstore and café where people of all ages discover new ideas, enjoy meaningful conversations, and revive the simple joy of books and togetherness.

Values

  • Community: We host events and programmes that bring neighbours together.
  • Accessibility: We strive to make books and events welcoming to everyone.
  • Sustainability: We support used books and local suppliers.
  • Curiosity: We champion new voices and diverse perspectives.

Community Programmes

Monthly reading circles, book exchanges, author signings and school outreach. See our Events page for upcoming dates.

Visit Us
