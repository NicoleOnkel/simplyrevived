About Simply Revived

Simply Revived began as a small neighbourhood bookstore with a simple aim: to rekindle the love of reading and build a local place where people can connect over stories and coffee.

Our History

Founded in 2015 by two book lovers, Simply Revived started on a modest street corner with second-hand books, a few armchairs and a kettle on. Over time the shop found its voice — hosting local author evenings, supporting reading circles for kids, and becoming a comfortable place where students and remote workers stop by for a quiet hour.

Mission

To inspire a love of reading and nurture community by offering a carefully curated selection of books and a warm café space where conversations and ideas flourish.

Vision

To be the go-to neighbourhood bookstore and café that celebrates stories, supports local writers, and creates memorable moments for readers of all ages.

Values

  • Community: We host events and programs that bring people together.
  • Accessibility: Books and events available to a wide and diverse audience.
  • Sustainability: We favour used books, local suppliers and low-waste packaging.
  • Curiosity: We champion new voices and diverse perspectives.

What We Offer

Alongside our bookshop you’ll find a cosy café corner serving freshly brewed coffee and baked goods — perfect for reading, meeting friends, or working quietly. We stock fiction, non-fiction, children’s books, local authors and a rotating selection of staff picks.

Community Programmes

Simply Revived runs monthly reading circles, school outreach events, and author signings. We also have a small book-exchange shelf encouraging book-sharing among neighbours.

Visit Us

We’re located at 123 Book Street, Cape Town. Drop in for a browse, a chat, or a slice of cake — we’d love to meet you.
